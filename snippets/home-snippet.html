If you find yourself in the White Mountains with an appetite for some authentic Thai Cuisine, you have come to the right place.

Mountain Thai Restaurant was once again voted ” The best Thai Restaurant in the White Mountains” by Market Surveys of America for 2016.

With over 17 years in business here in Lakeside, Mountain Thai is proud to be considered an icon in the area. That said, with Noy Garbeil (owner and cook), born and raised in Thailand, it is no wonder this place is still going strong.

So whether you’re looking for an extraordinary meal, or the casual atmosphere provided by this local husband and wife team, Mountain Thai is open five days a week (closed Monday and Tuesday), and happy to cook something up for you. We also offer cocktails, wines, and a full selection of Asian and Domestic Beers. Thai favorites such as Singha and Chang Beer as well as Mekhong Whiskey are always on hand to offer an authentic Thai experience! We have an extensive selection of bottled Asian beers including Tiger Beer and BierLao , Saigon, Sapporo Premium Draft is always on tap as well as Local Brews such as Oak Creek Nut Brown Ale and Black Horse Brewery’s Gold Dust Honey Lager, as well as $3.00 Shock top Drafts ’til 5 PM….. Hot and chilled Sake are also available.

We also carry a varied selection of Asian Market items such as curry pastes, rice noodles, bean thread noodles, sri racha, sweet chili sauce, Thai Tea and coffee, Tamarind, Kaffir Lime, lemon grass, Boba, rice paper wrappers, coconut milk, etc., etc. Come in and check it out, and if you don’t see what you’re looking for, just ask.
